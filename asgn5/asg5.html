>> NOTES FOR GRADER:
  • This project might take a little while to load, so please be patient!


    • >> INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Click to lock pointer, then:
  • Use WASD to move around the scene, Q/E to move up and down, R to reset camera position.
  • Use mouse to look around.
  • Press ESC to unlock pointer.


    • >> FEATURES:
  • Chain, fox, and feather particles are all custom textured 3D models, edited in Blender.
  • Background is a custom HDRI image I put together and rendered in Blender, and applied as a skybox.
  • SHELL TEXTURING! for the fur of the fox. Custom fur shader material with instanced mesh.
  • Particle system for the feathers.
  • Bloom post processing effect.

    • >> LIGHTS
  • One directional light pointing up from the bottom left.
  • One spot light for the red lighting on the shapes.
  • Ambient light for overall quality.
  • Modified materials in Blender to be emissive, which light other objects in the scene.
